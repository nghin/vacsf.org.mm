Foundational Truths

  • No More Condemnation
    • 1Therefore there is now no condemnation for those who are in Christ Jesus. 2For the law of the Spirit of life in Christ Jesus has set you free from the law of sin and of death (Romans 8:1).
  • Walk According to the Flesh
    • To walk by the flesh is to use the flesh, what it does or does not, to determine one's relationship with God.
  • How one falls from God's grace
    • You who are trying to be justified by the law have been alienated from Christ; you have fallen away from grace (Galatians 5:4)
  • The law does not bring righteousness
    • Therefore no one will be declared righteous in God's sight by the works of the law; rather, through the law we become conscious of our sin (Romans 3:20)
